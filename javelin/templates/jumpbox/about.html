Our rate

After we receive the requirements of your project, we will perform a detailed breakdown and estimate the total effort needed. The hourly rate we quote depends on a number of factors.

  • The pace: how much effort you require in a weekly iteration.
  • The manpower: number of developers to work on your project.
  • The scope: total days contracted. We provide a discount if you contract more.

We practice evidence-based scheduling so that both you and we have full view and control over the running cost and the progress of the project.
